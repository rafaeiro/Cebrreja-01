It was designed to be versatile, catering to a variety of purposes, and its different weights can be used to ensure visual consistency across various sizes. As a general guideline for the CeBRreja font, it is suggested to reduce a heavier weight by half the font size, two heavier weights for a third of the size, three heavier weights for a quarter of the size, and so on, following this logic. However, it's important to add a bit of size as needed to balance the perception of weight. Also, remember that, typically, these reductions benefit from additional letter spacing and an increase in line spacing.
